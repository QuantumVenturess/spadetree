Tutees

If you love doing anything but do not have the resources or knowledge to grow, become a tutee and join us so that we can help you learn what you love.

Tutors

If you have a passion and would like to teach your skill to a youth, become a tutor and join us in our mission to spread knowledge like leaves in the wind.

How it works for Tutees

Tell us what you are interested in and what you want to learn, anything from sports to art or DJing to Starcraft

Search for a tutor who is passionate about the topic you are interested in

Have your parent or guardian arrange a safe learning environment for you and your tutor

How it works for Tutors

Tell us what you are passionate about and what skills you would like to teach

Once selected as a tutor, choosing whether you want to teach once a year or everyday is your decision

Work with their parent or guardian to set up a time and place for you to start teaching your skills

Whether you want to learn something new or have a passion to share, becoming a part of the family will help grow our community and spread knowledge around the world.
